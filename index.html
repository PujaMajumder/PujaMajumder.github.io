Thanks for stopping by my research page! Currently I am working as a Research Associate at the Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center, New York, USA. In my current research, I seek to understand the diversity and versatility of defense strategies underlying the arms race between viruses and bacterial surveillance systems by focusing on cyclic-oligoadenylate (cOA) activated auxiliary CARF-effector proteins associated with the type-III CRISPR-Cas system. To do this, I routinely use cryo electron microscopy and X-ray crystallographic techniques. By training, I am a structural biologist with a doctoral dissertation on structure of membrane protein. To know more details, please visit the Research interest and the Publications pages. Happy browsing!
